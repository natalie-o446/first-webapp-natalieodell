The Team

We have many great athletes here at CCA. As of this season (spring 2025) there have been several school records that were broken by our competing athletes. We have also set a new record for how many athletes earned a new personal best this year as well. If you would like to join, come check us out! Practices are in the afternoon from 3:45PM to 5:45PM. We have different events such as Long Distance, Middle Distance, Short Distance/Sprints, Shotup and Discuss, Long Jump, Pole Vault, Triple Jump, and High Jump that are very welcoming squads who would be happy to have you!
