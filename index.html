CCA Track and Field Team

We are the Track and Field Team at CCA. We strive to improve our program each year through hard work and commitment to this team. We are able to achieve this through competing to the best of our abilities against other schools in our league. If you are thinking about trying out for our team, it is highly encouraged! There are no cuts for incoming freshman, but there are fun/competitive time stamps for returning altheles required in order to earn a spot on the team!
